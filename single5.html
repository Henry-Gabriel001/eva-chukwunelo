SUICIDE IS NEVER AN OPTION

june 1, 2025 Eva Chukwunelo

WHY DID I WANT TO KILL MYSELF?

Have you ever asked yourself this question when ever you attempt to commit suicide. suicide is never an option , Everything happens for a reason for every effect there is a specific cause. killing yourself in-order some problems of life is never a good decision rather stand out to fight it by setting out goals. This is why goals are important , goals allow you to control the direction of change in your life. goals assure that change in your life is predominantly in the direction that you want to go. goals gives you control over critical element of your life. Goals give you a greater sense of personal power and well-being. Examine your life carefully and notice the part of your life that causes you the greatest amount of stress ,anger, frustration , pain on regular basis. Resolve to make a decision in each of these areas, to either get in or get out, to do something or to stop whatever you are doing. whether it is job , a relationship or an investment of time, loan, money or emotions. Remember always that you are where you are because of yourself , because of your own choice and decisions.

Suicide is a deeply sensitive and complex issue that touches the lives of many individuals and families worldwide. It reflects a moment of despair, emotional pain, or a feeling of hopelessness. However, it is vital to remember that suicide is never the answer. There are always alternatives, support systems, and ways to find hope, even in the darkest times. The Truth About Suicide Many people contemplating suicide believe they are entirely alone and that their pain is insurmountable. But, often, feelings of hopelessness are temporary and can be alleviated through connection, understanding, and professional help. Reaching out is a crucial first step toward healing. Why Suicide Is Never an Option Life Can Change: Difficult circumstances, emotional pain, or mental health struggles can improve with time, support, and treatment. Help Is Available: There are resources, professionals, friends, and family who want to help and support you through tough times. Impact on Loved Ones: Suicide not only affects the individual but also leaves deep scars on family, friends, and the community. Your Potential: Every person has unique talents, dreams, and worth. No matter how bad things seem, your life matters. Strategies to Overcome Suicidal Thoughts Reach Out Talk to trusted friends, family members, or mental health professionals about what you’re experiencing. Sharing your feelings can provide relief and new perspectives. Seek Professional Help Counselors, therapists, and psychiatrists are trained to help navigate emotional pain. Therapy and medication can be effective in managing mental health issues. Create a Safety Plan Identify warning signs of emotional distress, and develop a plan to stay safe, including emergency contacts and comforting activities. Engage in Self-Care Regular exercise, proper nutrition, adequate sleep, and mindfulness practices can significantly improve mental well-being. Remember, You Are Not Alone Join support groups or communities where people share experiences and encouragement. Many have walked through similar darkness and found light again. Conclusion Suicide is never the solution. No matter how overwhelming your feelings may seem, help is always available, and hope can be rediscovered. Your life is valuable, and your presence in this world makes a difference. If you or someone you know is struggling, reach out today — there is support and a brighter future awaiting.
